What is Site Metrics?

Site Metrics is a dynamic application designed to simplify website traffic analysis. It provides real-time insights into key metrics such as total visits, traffic sources, bounce rates, session durations, and user locations.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower businesses and website owners with actionable data, enabling them to optimize their online presence and enhance user engagement.
