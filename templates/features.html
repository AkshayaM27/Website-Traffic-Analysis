Key Features of Site Metrics

Gain insights into your website's performance with these key metrics.

Total Visits

Track the total number of visits to your website and analyze trends over time.

Traffic Sources

Identify the origins of your visitors, such as search engines, social media, or direct visits.

Bounce Rates

Understand how users interact with your site and what percentage leave without engagement.

Session Duration

Measure how long visitors stay on your site and which pages hold their attention the most.
